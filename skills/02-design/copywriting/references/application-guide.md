# Copywriting Application Guide

## Channel Templates

### Cold Email

**Structure:** [CONTEXT] + [FRAMEWORK] + [CONSTRAINTS] + [OUTPUT]

```
Subject: [Curiosity-driven, <50 chars]

[Problem - 1-2 sentences identifying pain]

[Agitate - 1-2 sentences amplifying consequences]

[Solution - 1-2 sentences positioning your offer]

[CTA - Single, clear action]

[Signature]
```

**Specs:**
- Length: 75-150 words
- One CTA only
- No attachments in first touch
- Subject line triggers: curiosity, specificity, or personalization

**Framework Stack:** PAS + Curiosity + 1 Primal Desire

### Landing Page

**Structure:**

1. **Hero Section**
   - Headline: Core desire in <10 words
   - Subheadline: BAB in 2-3 sentences
   - CTA: Action-oriented

2. **Problem Section (PAS)**
   - 3-4 specific pain points
   - Emotional amplification
   - "Maybe you've tried..." (failed solutions)

3. **Solution Section (4 P's)**
   - Problem recap
   - Promise: Specific outcome + timeframe
   - Proof: Mini case study
   - Proposal: High-level offer

4. **How It Works**
   - 3-step process (simplify complexity)
   - Focus on transformation, not methodology

5. **Results/Proof**
   - Case studies (Star-Story-Solution)
   - Before/After format
   - Specific, measurable outcomes

6. **Investment**
   - Value equation breakdown
   - What's included
   - Lead with value, then price

7. **FAQ (Objection Handling)**
   - Address all 5 deep objections
   - NO MONEY, NO TRUST, NO URGENCY, NO NEED, NO AUTHORITY

8. **Final CTA**
   - Restate transformation
   - Address fear of wrong decision
   - Urgency/scarcity (if real)

**Specs by ticket:**
- Low ticket: 800-1,500 words
- Mid ticket: 1,500-2,500 words
- High ticket: 2,500-4,000 words

### Social Media Ads

**Structure (PAS condensed):**

```
[Hook - Stop scroll in 5 words]

[Problem - 1 sentence]

[Agitate - Emotional punch]

[Solution teaser]

[CTA with urgency]
```

**Platform specs:**
- Facebook: 125 chars primary, 40 chars headline
- Instagram: 125 chars, visual-first
- LinkedIn: 150 chars, professional tone
- Google: 30/30/90 char headlines/descriptions

**Always generate 5 variations:**
1. Fear-based (Loss Aversion)
2. Desire-based (BAB)
3. Curiosity-based (Open loop)
4. Status-based (Superiority)
5. Social Proof-based (Authority)

### Email Sequence

**Overall arc:** Educate → Inspire → Sell

**D.O.S.E. journey across sequence:**
- Emails 1-2: Build Oxytocin (trust, connection)
- Email 3: Trigger Dopamine (possibility, hope)
- Email 4: Address Cortisol (fear of wrong choice)
- Email 5: Dopamine surge (excitement to act)

**Per-email specs:**
- Length: 200-400 words
- One primary message
- One CTA (soft → hard progression)
- Handle one objection per email

**5-Email Welcome Sequence:**

| Email | Day | Framework | Focus | CTA |
|-------|-----|-----------|-------|-----|
| 1 | 0 | Reciprocity | Deliver value + bonus | Soft (reply) |
| 2 | 2 | Star-Story-Solution | Case study | Soft (read more) |
| 3 | 4 | FAB | Introduce offer | Medium (watch/learn) |
| 4 | 6 | Value Equation + Objection | Address investment | Medium (see details) |
| 5 | 8 | AIDA + Scarcity | Launch/deadline | Hard (buy now) |

### VSL / Video Script

**Structure (10-15 min):**

| Phase | Time | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| Hook | 0:00-0:15 | Pattern interrupt, promise |
| Problem | 0:15-3:00 | Amplify pain, shared experience |
| Story | 3:00-7:00 | Star-Story-Solution, credibility |
| Solution | 7:00-10:00 | Present offer, benefits |
| Proof | 10:00-12:00 | Testimonials, results, guarantees |
| Close | 12:00-15:00 | CTA, scarcity, stack value |

**Neurochemical arc:**
Cortisol spike (problem) → Oxytocin (story) → Dopamine (solution) → Serotonin (status) → Endorphin (relief/decision)

**Include:** Visual/editing cues for production team

### Website Copy (General)

**Homepage:**
- Hero: What you do + for whom + outcome
- Problem: Why they're here
- Solution: How you help
- Proof: Results, clients, testimonials
- CTA: Primary action

**About Page:**
- Origin story with vulnerability
- Credentials (not resume dump)
- Mission/values connection
- Team (if applicable)
- CTA: Continue journey

**Services/Product Pages:**
- FAB structure for each
- Three Brains: Features (neocortex) → Benefits (limbic) → Safety (reptilian)
- Social proof per service
- Clear pricing or "get quote"

## Tone Calibration

**Use 1-10 scale for each dimension:**

| Dimension | 1 | 10 |
|-----------|---|-----|
| Formality | Casual, conversational | Corporate, professional |
| Energy | Calm, measured | Excited, urgent |
| Empathy | Direct, factual | Warm, understanding |
| Authority | Peer, relatable | Expert, commanding |

**Industry defaults:**

| Industry | Formality | Energy | Empathy | Authority |
|----------|-----------|--------|---------|-----------|
| B2B SaaS | 6 | 5 | 6 | 8 |
| Healthcare | 7 | 4 | 9 | 9 |
| E-commerce | 4 | 7 | 5 | 5 |
| Finance | 8 | 5 | 7 | 9 |
| Coaching | 5 | 7 | 9 | 7 |
| Legal | 9 | 3 | 6 | 10 |
| Restaurant | 3 | 8 | 7 | 4 |
| Real Estate | 6 | 6 | 8 | 7 |

## Industry Adaptations

### B2B / SaaS
- Lead with ROI and efficiency
- Primal: #5 (Comfortable Living), #6 (Winning)
- Address: Implementation time, integration, support
- Proof: Case studies with metrics

### Healthcare / Dental
- Compliance-conscious language
- Primal: #3 (Freedom from Fear), #7 (Care for Others)
- Authority: Cite professional organizations
- Trust: Credentials, certifications, guarantees

### E-commerce / DTC
- Speed and simplicity
- Primal: #2 (Enjoyment), #5 (Comfort)
- Urgency: Shipping, stock, sales
- Social: Reviews, UGC, influencer proof

### Professional Services
- Relationship-focused
- Primal: #8 (Social Approval), #6 (Superiority)
- Trust: Track record, client roster
- Process: What working together looks like

### Coaching / Courses
- Transformation-focused
- Primal: #6 (Winning), #8 (Belonging)
- Story: Founder journey, student results
- Community: Belonging, support, accountability

## Quality Checklist

Before delivering any copy:

**Structure:**
- [ ] Clear single CTA (or progressive CTAs for long-form)
- [ ] Logical flow matching awareness stage
- [ ] Appropriate length for channel

**Psychology:**
- [ ] Primary primal desire identified and targeted
- [ ] At least 2 psychological triggers applied
- [ ] Emotional journey mapped (start → middle → end state)

**Proof:**
- [ ] Claims substantiated or qualified
- [ ] Social proof included where appropriate
- [ ] Objections preemptively addressed

**Ethics:**
- [ ] No false scarcity
- [ ] No exaggerated claims
- [ ] No exploitation of vulnerability
- [ ] Serves the reader's genuine interest

**Deliverable:**
- [ ] Primary copy version complete
- [ ] Framework reasoning documented
- [ ] 3 variations with distinct angles
- [ ] Tone matches brand/industry

## A/B Test Generation

When generating variations, test ONE variable per set:

**Headline tests:**
- Desire vs. Fear framing
- Specific number vs. general promise
- Question vs. statement

**CTA tests:**
- Action verb variation
- Urgency vs. benefit
- First person vs. second person

**Body tests:**
- Story-led vs. data-led
- Short vs. long
- Social proof placement

**Label each variation clearly:**
```
Variation A: Fear-based (Loss Aversion)
Variation B: Desire-based (Future Pacing)
Variation C: Status-based (Social Proof)
```
